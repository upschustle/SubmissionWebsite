Rules

1. Decide a question you want to practice, or click this card to choose questions from our Ideas

Choose Question from QUESTION GALLERY


Generally, a good question is short, but is related to a very deep topic, touching various point of soceity all at once. these questions have no right or wrong answer, but how you express your view point is Extremely important!

Best way to choose a question according to me is decide which topic in last one week have you heard in news globally, Then Choose the topic you know the LEAST about!!! This helps.
2. Write your answer on a sheet of paper or a big register. (Preferable size is A4 Sheets)

Avoid using small notebooks or bad paper as it effects your quality of writing. White pages are not necessary, use any quality of paper/Notebook, But size of that thing is important!

I also prefer registers (White paper or Grey paper also), as they are big enough on single page to write long answer and I can see more text on one sheet, so less time wasted in Turning Pages!!
3. Write Page number on each page on top in BIG DIGITS

Page number is extremely important, since it will help you measure mentally of how much text you have written, Plus it helps me better navigate your answers

Take a sketchpen and write 'Page 1' and circle it, on TOP of the sheet.
4. Take photos of each page of answer, with the Question clearly written on top of the first page

Exactly as you would write an answer in mains, Before starting the answer you write the question and underline it. This is basic format for better presentation

The photos must be clear and NOT shaky, please use a friend's phone if your phone clicks blur photos. Keep phone on a glass table and the your answer below the table, this way you need not to hold the phone and it is EASY!
